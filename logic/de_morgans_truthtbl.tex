\section{De Morgan's for Three in a Truth Table}

 Use a truth table to show that the following are logically equivalent:
\[
\neg(p \vee q \vee r) \equiv \neg p \wedge \neg q \wedge \neg r
\]

To prove this, we construct a truth table for both sides and verify that they produce identical truth values for all combinations of $p$, $q$, and $r$.

\vspace{1em}

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{ccc|c|c|ccc|c}
\toprule
$p$ & $q$ & $r$ & $p \vee q \vee r$ & $\neg(p \vee q \vee r)$ & $\neg p$ & $\neg q$ & $\neg r$ & $\neg p \wedge \neg q \wedge \neg r$ \\
\midrule
T & T & T & T & F & F & F & F & F \\
T & T & F & T & F & F & F & T & F \\
T & F & T & T & F & F & T & F & F \\
T & F & F & T & F & F & T & T & F \\
F & T & T & T & F & T & F & F & F \\
F & T & F & T & F & T & F & T & F \\
F & F & T & T & F & T & T & F & F \\
F & F & F & F & T & T & T & T & T \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\vspace{1em}

Since the columns for $\neg(p \vee q \vee r)$ and $\neg p \wedge \neg q \wedge \neg r$ are identical for all eight rows, we conclude:
\[
\neg(p \vee q \vee r) \equiv \neg p \wedge \neg q \wedge \neg r \qquad \square
\]
